Candle Holder

Upcycle small glass jars into beautiful candle holders for your room.

Steps:

  1. Find a clean glass jar (small ones work best).
  2. Decorate the outside with paint, lace, or ribbons.
  3. Place a small candle or LED tealight inside.
  4. Add sand, pebbles, or dried flowers for extra style.
  5. Light it up and enjoy the cozy glow!
