Wooden Photo Frame

Make a beautiful photo frame using simple wooden sticks and glue—great for gifts or home decor!

Steps:

  1. Gather 4 wooden popsicle sticks (or thin wooden slats).
  2. Arrange them in a square or rectangle shape to form a frame.
  3. Glue the corners together and let it dry completely.
  4. Decorate with paint, buttons, shells, or stickers.
  5. Attach your favorite photo behind the frame and display it!
